Roland Jung holds a B.Sc (2013) and M.Sc (2015) in Computer Science from the University of Applied Sciences Upper Austria Hagenberg. He completed his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Klagenfurt in 2023. 
Jung began his career as a junior scientist with the Autonomous Systems group at the Austrian Institute of Technology (AIT) in Vienna from 2015 to 2017. Subsequently, he joined the Karl Popper School on Networked Autonomous Aerial Vehicles at the University of Klagenfurt, Austria, where he worked from 2017 to 2021. Since 2021, he has been a member of the Control of Networked Systems Group at the University of Klagenfurt.   
His research is primarily concerned with multi-robot (collaborative) state estimation focusing on true modularity and distributed computation and integration of new mutal sensing capbilities.
